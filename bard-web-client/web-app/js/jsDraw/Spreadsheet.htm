JSDraw2.Table - Intelligent Chemical Structure Spreadsheet

This is a demo to create an intelligent chemical structure table. Here is what you can play with it

  1. Add row
  2. Modify columns
  3. Double-click cells to edit them
  4. Molecular Formula and Molecular Weight are calculated automatically
  5. Effective Mass and Effective Moles are automatcially calculated based on Sample Mass and Purity
